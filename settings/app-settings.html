Is this your personal device, or is it shared?

If you trust this device, the app will keep a copy of your data on this device for use when you are offline or when you have a slow connection. This is the default behavior.

If this is a shared device, it is recommended to turn this feature off.

The app must be refreshed before this change can be applied!
